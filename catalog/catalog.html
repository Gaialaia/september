You can order tasty confectionary:

  • muffin

    • Muffin is a line of pastries from the romantic land of France.
    The cake was originally called Moufflet, using savory filling (vegetables, bacon,…)
    and sweet filling (jam filling, fruits, nuts,…), often enjoyed as breakfast,
    afternoon snacks or given as a gift for a loved one.

  • cupcake

    • Cupcake is a miniature version of cream cake, usually without a filling or a sweet filling.
    Its core is soft, light, sweet and has a layer of cream on top for decoration.
    When baked, it is put in a small paper cup instead of in a large cake mold like muffin.
    This type of cake is often enjoyed at parties as desserts.

  • nougat

    • Nougat is a family of confections made with sugar or honey, roasted nuts
    (almonds, walnuts, pistachios, hazelnuts, and macadamia nuts are common), whipped egg whites,
    and sometimes chopped candied fruit.
